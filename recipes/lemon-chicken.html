Easy 35 Minute Lemon Chicken Recipe

A Simple Recipe Anybody Can Make

In this recipe, you will learn how to cook a delicious lemon chicken meal. This recipe is perfect for those who are low on time and would like a quick bite to eat.

Ingredient List

  • 2 1/2 tbsp - Olive Oil
  • 2 - Broccoli Heads
  • 2 - Lemons
  • 2 ibs - Chicken Thighs
  • 3 - Garlic Cloves
  • 1/3 cup - Honey
  • Chicken Broth
  • 1 1/2 tbsp - Soy Sauce
  • Rice

Cooking Instructions

  1. Cut and dice broccoli heads. Cut lemons into halves and squeeze them onto a bowl.
  2. Before continuing further, begin cooking your rice.
  3. Cut and dice your chicken.
  4. To make the sauce, get your bowl of lemon juice, add chicken broth, garlic paste, honey, soy sauce, and then mix.
  5. Next, saute your cut up broccoli for 3-4 minutes. Make sure the broccoli has a golden brown color. Lastly, add 80 mL of water for steaming.
  6. Lastly, cook your chicken for 10 minutes before adding the sauce. Afterwards, mix around the sauce and the chicken until cooked to your liking.
  7. Now, it's time to eat!
